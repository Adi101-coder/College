Our Certifications & Online Programs For 2024

At Harvard University, we offer a wide array of certification and online programs designed to meet the needs of diverse learners around the globe. Our programs are created and delivered by esteemed faculty members, ensuring the highest standards of academic excellence and real-world applicability.


Pursue a Harvard degree from anywhere in the world. Our online degree programs offer the same rigorous curriculum and are taught by the same world-class faculty as our on-campus programs. Students have the flexibility to balance their studies with personal and professional commitments.


For those looking for a more concise learning experience, our microcredentials and short courses provide focused instruction on specific topics. These programs are ideal for professionals seeking to gain expertise in a new area or stay current with industry trends.


Our online programs are designed to be interactive and engaging, utilizing the latest in educational technology. Students will have access to a wealth of resources, including video lectures, interactive assignments, and virtual office hours with instructors. Additionally, our online community allows students to network and collaborate with peers from around the world.
